Introduction to Computer Vision

What is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is the science of teaching a computer how to interpret images. For computers to emulate visual perception in humans and be able to "see", one must first understand how human vision works. This is exactly what researchers aimed to do starting in the 1950s. They began to gain deeper understandings of the inner workings of the human eye.
