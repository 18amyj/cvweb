What is computer vision?

Computer vision is the science of teaching a computer how to perceive, process, and interpret visual data such as images and videos.

The goal of computer vision is to bridge the gap between pixels and meaning.

However, before computers could emulate visual perception in humans and be able to "see," researchers had to explore and unravel the inner workings of the human eye. Their findings would set the stage for groundbreaking discoveries in computer vision.
